4Ps of Innovation Challenge

Introduction

Welcome to the 4Ps of Innovation Challenge. In this game, you will learn about the four dimensions of innovation identified by Bessant and Tidd (2013): Product, Process, Position, and Paradigm.

Product Innovation focuses on creating or significantly improving products or services. Process Innovation looks at new ways of producing or delivering these offerings. Position Innovation involves changing how a product or service is perceived or marketed, while Paradigm Innovation means shifting the underlying business model or assumptions.

We will also distinguish between Incremental (small-scale) and Radical (major) changes in each area. For example, adding a new flavour to an existing pastry line is incremental, while adopting 3D-printed pastries might be radical.
